Appointments?

Please try to arrive five minutes before your salon appointment to ensure that our team members are able to accommodate future appointments in a timely fashion.

We also offer an extensive free beverage menu and free WiFi during your visit. While you wait, feel free to take a look at our retail area or peruse through a magazine.

*Important* If you are late for your appointment we will try to accommodate you with the time remaining, but it is possible that your appointment may have to be rescheduled for a later date or time.

Reservations?

Our services are in high demand on weeknights, weekends and holidays. We recommend making reservations in advance by either calling (731) 424-9346. If there is any reason you’d like to have another team member, please let Linda Cox (Owner) know and she will be more than happy to take care of you.

Cancellation/ No-Show?

There may be occasions when you’ll need to cancel an appointment that you have made. If that’s the case, please try to let us know within 24 hours so we can open up our books to other clients that need in to visit us.
You can cancel your appointment by calling the salon or contacting us before your appointment.

Not showing up for a scheduled appointment greatly inconveniences your service provider as well as other clients who may need an appointment.

Pricing?

We make every effort to keep pricing on the website and on our menus up to date. All pricing is a starting rate and may vary depending on the needs of the client.
Pricing is subject to change.

Payment Methods?

We require payment at the time of your service. Feel free to use any of the following methods to pay for your service:

  • Visa, MasterCard, Discover, or American Express

  • Debit

  • Cash or Check

Returns & Exchanges?

If you are not satisfied with a product you purchased, please bring it back within 14 days of the original purchase date for an exchange, account credit, or refund.

Service Guarantee?

If you are unsatisfied with your service for any reason, please let us know within seven business days of your appointment, and we will schedule you for a redo. If you’re still noy happy, please ask for the owner, so she can arrange for a refund of the service price plus tax.


Health Concerns?

Please make sure you let us know if you are allergic to aspirin or salicylates, or any other medical issues. This will ensure we safely preform your requested services.
