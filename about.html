Humble Beginnings

Vviolet Hair Care Products was established in 1992. It is a black-owned company with hair products formulated for all textures of hair. Vviolet Hair Care Products are especially made for the most sensitive scalps and restoration of damaged hair.

All Vviolet Hair Care Products are fully guaranteed. They are laboratory developed by some of the best chemists, but the most important aspect of Vviolet Hair Care Products development is that they are salon-tested to ensure that they satisfy various textures of hair and styles our customers want.

Vviolet Hair Salon is currently headquartered in Tennessee, but products are only sold to professional salons through qualified distributors in California, Texas, Ohio, Mississippi, Tennessee, Louisiana, Colorado, Oklahoma, Pennsylvania, Alabama, and Missouri.

Call us at (731) 424-9346 or email us for information about distributors in your area. We appreciate and thank you for your patronage and are confident that once you have tried Vviolet Hair Care Products, you will recommend it to everyone you know.

The Founder

Linda Cox

It has been a God-given talent, a love for beauty, persistence, determination and trust in God that have gotten Linda B. Cox where she is today. Cox is the CEO of Vviolet Hair Salon and the Vviolet Hair Care Product, which began in 1994 and ships nationwide.

Cox grew up in Humboldt, TN., where she graduated from Stigall High School. She attended Jackson State Community College, Victor's Business College in Memphis, and the Nashville campus of the Miami School of Beauty.

Having been at the College Street location for over 30 years, Cox said it has been wonderful being a business owner, even though it is hard work. Cox has also received praise from other women in the beauty field who she has inspired to be business owners.
